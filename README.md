# Resolv Corporation — Performance Dashboard (Phase 1)
### Market & Operational Analytics | Internship Project | 2026

---

## Project Overview

This project was completed as part of a Market and Operational Analytics internship with Resolv Corporation, a US-based manufacturer and distributor of fine chemicals, dyes, and dyestuffs supplying customers across 47 countries.

The objective was to design and build a complete internal performance management system around three strategic business pillars — brand identity, customer rejection management, and competitive pricing intelligence — and deliver it as a set of professional analytical outputs for leadership review.

---

## The Business Problem

Resolv's management identified three areas where structured data analysis was missing:

1. **Brand strength** was not being measured consistently across quarters
2. **Customer rejections** were costing the company money but the root causes were not being tracked
3. **Competitive pricing** challenges were happening but the sales team had no structured intelligence to respond

## Key Findings

- Customer rejection rate dropped from **5.8% to 1.8%** over five quarters — a 69% reduction driven by the pre-shipment sample program
- The sample program prevented an estimated **$344,400** in rejection costs over five quarters
- **87%** of customers who received cheaper competitor offers were successfully retained
- **All six brand metrics** exceeded their Q1 2026 targets simultaneously for the first time
- Net Promoter Score reached **68** — up 7 points from the previous quarter, above the 65+ target

---

## Three Strategic Pillars

### Pillar 1 — Branding
Tracked NPS, name recognition, quality consistency, on-time delivery, CSAT, and brand recall across five consecutive quarters. All six metrics showed consistent improvement.

### Pillar 2 — Customer Rejection Management
Analyzed a five-quarter trend showing direct correlation between sample program volume and rejection rate reduction. Built root cause breakdown and sample program effectiveness tables.

### Pillar 3 — Competitive Pricing Intelligence
Mapped 31 competitive price challenges in Q1 2026. Analyzed price gap by customer tier, retention lever effectiveness, and competitor win rates.

---

## Methodology

Data was collected from four sources: CRM system, ERP order management, quarterly customer surveys, and sales team field reports. A four-stage process was applied — collection, validation, analysis, and reporting. All figures in this project are illustrative placeholder data designed to demonstrate the framework. In production, all data would come from Resolv's live systems.
